The Wellful brand began with the name, which connected with our mission to build the first health insurance company you love.

{{ logo }}
{%- include wellful-hearts-animation.svg -%}

Following that, I developed the Wellful "w" glyph as the primary iconographic identifier for the brand.

It consists of two black hearts intersecting with a white highlight. The glyph reads immediately as a soft, rounded lowercase "w", whereas the heart forms appear to the viewer more gradually.
